How to Flash Custom Firmware on Antminer L7

Introduction:
Flashing custom firmware on your Antminer L7 can significantly improve performance, stability, and profitability. This guide will walk you through the entire process step-by-step.

Benefits of Custom Firmware:

  • Higher hashrate output
  • Better power efficiency
  • Advanced monitoring tools
  • More control over fan and voltage settings

Step-by-Step Flashing Guide:

  1. Download the Vnish L7 firmware from the official site or GitHub.
  2. Connect your miner to the same network as your computer.
  3. Access your miner's IP via web browser.
  4. Log in to the miner dashboard (default user: root / password: root).
  5. Navigate to the firmware upgrade section.
  6. Upload the .tar.gz firmware file and click “Flash”.
  7. Wait for the miner to reboot and verify successful installation.

Tips:

  • Always back up your current settings.
  • Ensure you have a stable power supply.
  • Don’t interrupt the flashing process once it starts.

Conclusion:
Custom firmware like Vnish can unlock your L7’s full potential — just follow the process carefully and enjoy optimized mining.
